Double Fudge Brownie Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 box brownie mix
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup Mazola® Corn Oil
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine brownie mix and flour in a mixing bowl. Stir in eggs and oil; blend thoroughly.
  3. Dough will be stiff. Add chocolate chips.
  4. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto greased baking sheets.
  5. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes; cool on rack.
